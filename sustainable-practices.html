Sustainable Practices

Composting is a natural process that turns organic waste, such as food scraps and yard trimmings, into nutrient-rich soil conditioner. By composting organic waste instead of sending it to the landfill, homeowners can reduce methane emissions, improve soil health, and reduce the need for chemical fertilizers. Compost can be used in gardens, landscaping, and potted plants to enrich the soil and promote healthy plant growth.
